About the BU Open House

Discover the purpose of the event and what you can expect as an attendee.

Event Purpose

The BU Open House event is a unique opportunity for prospective students, parents, and the community to explore Bangkok University. We aim to showcase our diverse programs, innovative teaching methods, and the vibrant campus life that defines our university.

Join us to:

  • Meet industry experts and faculty members
  • Attend engaging workshops and seminars
  • Network with like-minded individuals
  • Explore innovative projects and technologies developed by our students

Don't miss out on this chance to learn more about your future at Bangkok University!
